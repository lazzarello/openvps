Bandwidth Limit

This page allows you to set outgoing bandwidth limit. If you do not expect high traffic on your VPS, it is a good idea to set a low bandwidth limit (e.g. 256Kbit). A low bandwidth limit will protect you from unanticipated high bandwidth charges.



Outgoing Bandwidth Limit:



Note that setting a high limit does not guarantee you the bandwidth. Actual throughput is subject to various complex Internet networking conditions that are often beyond our control.
